About

Welcome to Phoenix Community Radio. We are Goole's premier internet radio service. We were formed in July 2022 by a band of volunteers dedicated to lifting community aspirations and providing entertainment.

Our volunteer presenters bring you shows ranging from classic rock, to indie music, to dance tunes, and everything in between. We also do interviews, news, and sports shows where we bring community members together to shine a light on their wonderful work.

We are based in Carlisle House, Goole, East Yorkshire but our shows reach across the UK. If you would like to help support a show through sponsorship or even join as a volunteer presenter please reach out to us via email or use the contact form below. You can also reach us via our Facebook page
